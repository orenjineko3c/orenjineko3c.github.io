Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to buy $OREN?

    You can buy $OREN on a Binance Smart Chain (BSC), you need wallet to connect BSC.

  • How to get wallet and set up BSC?

    first you need wallet and you can Download the official Metamask extension for Google Chrome from metamask.io if you using desktop and follow the steps to create an account. On Mobile Download the official TrustWallet app from trustwallet.com for your mobile device. Don't forget to save your recovery phrase and never share it with anyone. to setp up BSC on wallet you can google it.

  • Wallet ready connect to BSC, what next?

    Purchase on Exchange such as Binance.com. You will need this BNB to purchase your Orenjineko ($OREN). Once you have obtained your BNB send it to your wallet address as BNB BEP20.

  • BNB on Wallet done, next?

    Visit pancakeswap.finance connect your wallet, click swap. Select BNB as the top option and paste our contract address (0xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx) into the bottom selection..

  • How does the OREN reward system work? Where do I get the rewards?

    For each active user transaction, a $OREN reward of 5% is distributed among all $OREN holders. Just buy, keep $OREN in your wallet and get rewards💰
